What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is a step-by-step procedure used to solve a problem or perform a task.

Algorithms are used in programming, mathematics, and everyday activities such as cooking, driving, or sending messages.

Main Characteristics of an Algorithm

1. Input
   - An algorithm accepts zero or more inputs.

2. Output
   - It produces at least one output or result.

3. Definiteness
   - Every step must be clear and unambiguous.

4. Finiteness
   - The algorithm must end after a finite number of steps.

5. Effectiveness
   - Each step must be simple and executable.

6. Correctness
   - The algorithm should produce the correct result for valid input.
